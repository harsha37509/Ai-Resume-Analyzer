About AI Resume Analyzer

The AI Resume Analyzer is an advanced platform designed to help job seekers and recruiters make the most of resume analysis and candidate ranking. Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to provide actionable insights for resume optimization and fair candidate evaluation.

Our Mission

To empower professionals by providing intelligent, data-driven resume analysis tools that help them present their qualifications in the best possible light, while enabling recruiters to identify top talent efficiently.

Help & FAQs

How do I upload a resume?

Go to the Home page, select either "Single Resume Analysis" or "Multi-Resume Ranking", and click the file input area or drag and drop your resume file (PDF or DOCX format).

What file formats are supported?

We support both PDF and DOCX (Word) formatted resumes. Ensure your file is not too large (max 10MB) and contains readable text.

How is the match score calculated?

The match score (0-100%) is calculated using a hybrid approach combining keyword matching (50%) and specific skill matching (50%) against the job description you provide.

Is my resume data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. Your resume data is not stored permanently on our servers and is only processed for analysis during your session.

Can I compare multiple candidates?

Yes! Use the "Multi-Resume Ranking" feature to upload up to 100 resumes and compare them side-by-side with detailed metrics and visualizations.
